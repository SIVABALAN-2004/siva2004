PAROTTA

Parotta is a popular South Indian flatbread that originated in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is also commonly consumed in Kerala and parts of Karnataka. Parotta is known for its flaky, layered texture, which is achieved through a unique kneading and folding technique.
